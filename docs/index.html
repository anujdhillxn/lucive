Lucive is an application designed to help users take control of their screen time.

With Lucive, users form duos to keep each other accountable and stay on track with their digital detox goals.

Users can set various rules like hourly limit, daily limit, session limit, and 10 seconds startup delay.

Any change to the rule requires an approval from the partner of the user.
